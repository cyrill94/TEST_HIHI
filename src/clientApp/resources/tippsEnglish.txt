Action cards that allow further action should be played first.
Only play attack cards when you have an excess of actions left to take.
The reward of gold and silver cards increases over time.
In general, the expensive cards are worth their money.
It is not recommended to own the same card multiple times.
Buying Point cards during the first rounds isn't rational.
Point cards slow down the game, while Money cards open up new possibilities.
If you feel superior to your opponent, try to end the game before he recovers.
